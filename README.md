A fighting game between two players on stages with platforms.
There's 3 different characters (Mario, ninja, tank), each of them with unique special attacks, weight, height, etc.
A match pf three stocks (lifes) where you you deal damage to your opponent. The higher your percentage (the damage you took) the stronger the knockback of your opponents' attacks.
You can choose between the 3 different characters and 3 different stages with platforms.
If you need, the instuctions of the controls are always visual under the screen of the game itself.
